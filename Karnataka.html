Dharmasthala Temple, devoted to the Hindu god Manjunatha. Some other temples and shrines are dedicated to Ammanavaru, Chandranath and the Dharma Daivas (guardian spirits of Dharma) — Kalarahu, Kalarkayi, Kumaraswamy and Kanyakumari. The river that flows near Dharmasthala is the Netravati River. It is considered one of the holy rivers of India and is also known as Netravathi Nadi.
